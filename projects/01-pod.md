## Thesis
- POD is a high-beta decentralized AI compute/data-generation option: if Dolphin proves real paid demand and automatic POD buybacks, the ~$15M circulating market cap screens low versus the scale of claimed GPU resources.
- On FDV, the bar is much higher: at ~$168M FDV, POD needs roughly `$8.4M-$16.8M` annualized revenue to screen at 20x-10x sales, or about `$23k-$46k/day`.
- The core underwriting variable is not whether GPUs exist; it is whether live paid workloads create transparent, durable buybacks.
