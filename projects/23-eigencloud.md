# EIGEN / EigenCloud

## Overview

EigenCloud is the EigenLayer/Eigen Labs team's attempt to extend the original restaking/AVS thesis into a **verifiable cloud for autonomous agents**. The core pitch is that agents will need memory, identity, execution receipts, and economic accountability before they can safely hold money, operate businesses, or coordinate high-stakes workflows.

- **Token:** EIGEN. Intake contract: `0xec53bf9167f50cdeb3ae105f56099aaab9061f83` — verify against official EigenLayer/EigenCloud token pages before use.
- **Core product / system:** EigenCloud, EigenCompute / verifiable compute, EigenDA, AgentKit, verifiable memory/agent coordination, and EIGEN-secured accountability / slashing rails.
- **Team / public contributors:** `@eigencloud` is the main current product account. `@EigenLayer`, `@eigenlabs`, `@sreeramkannan`, and `@gajeshnaik` are useful related accounts for protocol, labs, founder, and product/builder context.
- **Positioning:** a crypto-AI infrastructure bet that AI agents become economic actors, but need cryptographic receipts plus EIGEN-backed consequences to be trusted.

### Hermes take

> EigenCloud is a more interesting version of EigenLayer than the old generic-restaking story. The good setup is clear: AI agents need trust, receipts, rights, wallets, memory, and consequences; EIGEN already has a cryptoeconomic security brand; EigenCloud can turn that into a real product narrative. The hard part is proving fees and usage before the market writes it off as another restaking rebrand. For EIGEN, watch fee capture and buyback/burn evidence, not just agent philosophy.
