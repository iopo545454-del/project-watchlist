Latest scan notes

  • 2026-07-23 — Dedicated verification located the public machine-readable Organic Jobs dashboard. From July 16 06:00 through July 23 06:00 UTC it reported 2,214 submitted jobs, 2,055 succeeded, 158 failed and 211,940 accepted booked compute-seconds (~58.87 hours). 1,931 / 87.22% were health probes; the only other active label, GS, sent 283 jobs, of which 124 succeeded and 158 failed. sn17, KK and AC showed none. The metagraph showed 16 emission-bearing neurons split evenly between miners and validators, but no billing, direct-payment revenue, customer identity, inventory or utilization. Machine-readable activity is confirmed; treating it as paid customer revenue is disproven.

Thesis

  • Compute Horde is cheap optionality rather than a proven inference neo-cloud. It could re-rate if it shows real external jobs, active GPU utilization, or a clear route into Bittensor's AI compute demand.
  • Until then, it ranks behind Chutes/Targon/Lium/Akash on product evidence and revenue quality.
