Overview

  • USD.AI is a GPU/AI-infrastructure-backed synthetic dollar / credit protocol.
  • Products include USDAI and yield-bearing sUSDAI.
  • Governance/token: CHIP.
  • Thesis: on-chain credit market for AI compute/GPU financing; “interest rate of AI.”
  • Builder: Permian Labs.
